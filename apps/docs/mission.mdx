**Strata Sync exists to make local-first sync a solved problem for TypeScript developers.**

This is not a research project. It is not a proof of concept. Strata Sync is a production-ready sync engine that handles the hard parts so you ship the product instead of the plumbing.

## What "solved" means

You should not need to research CRDTs to ship a collaborative feature. You should not need to build an offline queue to make your save button work. You should not need to write conflict resolution logic for every model in your app.

The sync engine should be as invisible as the garbage collector. You define your models, bind them to your UI, and everything stays in sync across clients, tabs, and sessions. That is the bar.

## Who we serve

Product engineers building with TypeScript, React, and Next.js. Small teams that cannot afford to build sync infrastructure from scratch. Companies building apps where local-first matters: project management, collaborative documents, design tools, CRM, team chat.

If you reach for `useEffect` and `fetch` today, you should reach for Strata Sync instead.

## What we do not do

We do not replace your database. We replicate it. Your Postgres stays exactly where it is.

We do not own your deployment. We provide the engine. You run it on your infrastructure, behind your auth, inside your network.

We do not lock you in. Every adapter is swappable. Every byte of code is open-source. You can replace the storage layer, the transport layer, or the conflict resolution strategy without forking the project.

See our [Product Vision](product-vision) for where we are heading.
